WELCOME


The heart-beat of a neighbourhood is the interaction and cooperation between and among neighbours. This can be achieved through shared experiences such events and public participation forums.

Mtaa Inc. identified a gap in communication of activities taking place in neighbourhoods. This is because of lack of meaningful connections that limit prior notifications. Interactions depend on the existence of connections, networks and shared experiences.

Lack of awareness of events such as public meetings and resident meetups breeds feelings of being disconnected and lonely. Word of mouth is unverifiable and posters litter the environment.

Our solution therefore is Mtaa, a safe and credible online platform where members of a neighbourhood can sign up to:

  • View Community Events .
  • Receive notification to members via email.
  • Give Feedback through suggestions and general enquiries.

Mtaa will help close this information gap to help ignite interaction among neighbours
