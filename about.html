Mtaa

People are the building blocks of neighbourhoods. The heart-beat of a neighbourhood is the interaction and cooperation between and among neighbours. We identified a gap in communication of activities taking place in neighbourhoods. This is as a result of a lack of meaningful connections therefore limiting prior notifications. Interactions depend on the existence of connections, networks and shared experiences.

Lack of awareness of events such as public meetings and resident meetups breeds feelings of being disconnected and lonely. Word of mouth is unverifiable and posters litter the emvironment.

Our solution therefore is Mtaa, a safe and credible online platform where members of a neighbourhood can sign up to:

  • View Community Events.
  • Sned notification to members via email.
  • Give feedback through suggestions and general enquiries.

Mtaa will help close this information gap thus help generate cooperation among neighbourhoods.
