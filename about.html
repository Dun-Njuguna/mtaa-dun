Mtaa "{Swahili for Neighbourhood}" is the neighbourhood you live in, raise your children and for some, earn their livelihoods. Each is unique with its own history, culture, physical setting and people. Your desire is to know what is happening in your Mtaa.

Do you wish to be more connected to your neighbours?

Do you feel disconnected from your neighbours?

Are you ever always behind the events and activities in your neighbourhood?

What is Mtaa?

Mtaa is the safe and credible platform that connects you to your neighbours through the events, activities and meetups in your mtaa.

Get to meet up with your neighbours and share experiences and build a network in your neighbourhood. Know your neighbours. Sign up, log in and join your neighbours in a local run, end-year party or birthday

Who we target?

Do you wish to know what is happening in your Mtaa? Do you wish and long to connect with your neighbours through? Do you wish to create meaningful connections through shared experiences that extend your network?

Mtaa will help close this gap for you by igniting interactions among neighbour.

Why?

Your need for connection and security is a part of the natural law. The heart-beat of a neighbourhood is the interaction and cooperation between and among neighbours.

A gap in communication of events and activities taking place exists because of lack of meaningful connections that limit prior notifications. This lack of awareness breeds feelings of being disconnected and lonely yet word of mouth is unveriable and posters litter th environment.
